Check Out Our Newest Release!

We are proud to announce the latest CD release from Grumpy Puppy Productions, Fishin' Hole. Richard F. Howard is one of the first artists to sign with Grumpy Puppy. We immediately got to work producing this awesome CD. The title song is a rocking little tune about the joys of fishing. The rest of the CD is filled with catchy tunes that are bound to climb the folk charts here in Texas. Richard has been touring extensively in the local area preparing for his first nation-wide tour which kicks off in January. Stay tuned for more news on Richard's amazing journey with Grumpy Puppy Productions.

The Alamo City Folk Festival is coming in October!

Mark your calendars! From Friday, October 14th through Sunday, October 16th we will be celebrating Folk music and Folk culture at Ratama Park. There will be live music (of course!) all day and night for three solid days!

Grumpy Puppy Productions is helping sponsor the event and we have four of our artists performing, including the headliners, "Uncle Sugar!" and "Possum Bait!"

Come out and bring the kids too! There will be carnival rides and a petting zoo! There will be lots of food and drinks also. So come on out to Ratama Park on October 14-16 from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM all three days!

Arduous Is Back In The Studio!

One of our most popular acts, Arduous! is back in the studio working on their fourth album. This CD is destined to be a classic. Produced by Marvin Dykhuis, the songs are all original compositions written by some of the hottest new Texas songwriters. The title song, "Infinity" was written by Austin songwriter, Adam Belsky.

Stay tuned and we'll let you know when Infinity is ready for purchase.

Grumpy Puppy Productions Signs The Mavens!

You heard that right folks! San Antonio's biggest folk act is now a member of the GPP family! We are so excited!

Grumpy Puppy Productions will be producing the Mavens next album starting in July so look for that to hit the stores sometime in November. We will also be setting up a mini-tour to promote the album locally. That will be in September. Then in January the Mavens hit the road and begin touring nation-wide! We cannot wait! Lawrence Mills has signed on to act as tour manager. Lawrence has thirty years experience managing big tours like this, so we know it's going to be a huge success. Stay tuned because as we get closer to the release date we will be putting out the calendar of appearances so you can buy your tickets.

New Studio Equipment!

Grumpy Puppy Productions is a full-service record label. We handle all of our artists professional needs from recording to touring. Therefore, it's pretty exciting when something happens to make that service even better. This week our recording studio got a major facelift! We were able to aquire a NEVE 8078 mixing board from a facility in California and ship it out here to be installed in our Studio-B. We also got some new microphones and two racks full of processors. Our engineers are having a blast trying all this new stuff out. We can't wait to hear how it sounds! Y'all stay tuned!
